About Us

Curing Child Hunger

The Georgia Tech Engineering for Social Innovation Center has partnered with EMSquared to eliminate childhood malnutrition around the world. To combat this global epidemic, EMSquared manufactures the Hope Bar. This sweet, delicious bar contains all the nutrition, vitamins, minerals, protein, and fat that a child needs for an entire day. Because the target population typically cannot afford to purchase the bars, EMSquared developed a sustainable funding model that enables the donation of two bars for every one bar that is purchased.

Reboot Your Life

Use Hope Bars to reboot your life! There are 3 rules for rebooting (15 days).

  1. Carbohydrates: Eliminate grains, sugars, and most fruits
  2. Protein: Moderate your intake - on average 15 grams per meal (Too much protein will also convert to sugar)
  3. Fat: Increase your intake of healthy fats
